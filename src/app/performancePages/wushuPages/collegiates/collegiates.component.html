Collegiates

Collegiates is an annual Wushu competition organized by the Collegiate Wushu Committee, which is a group of college Wushu club leaders from around the nation lead by some club coaches and competition judges, that vote on and discuss when and where to host the competition as well as any changes to rules and scoring that need to be made.

Collegiates 2017: UPitt

The 2017 competition at the University of Pittsburg was the first Collegiates I attended, where I received one gold medal and two silvers. Check out these videos of my performances. (video credits to Mitchell Levasseur)
